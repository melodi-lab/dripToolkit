In the Cygwin setup (note, you can rerun setup to install/update
additional packages), install:
pkg-config
ghostscript
libfreetype-devel
libpng-devel
python-gtk2.0
libgtk2.0-devel
gcc-g++
openbox
python-numpy
python-setuptools

Restarting Cygwin, run:
easy_install-2.7 https://github.com/matplotlib/matplotlib/archive/master.zip

This may take some time to build.  To confirm matplotlib was built correctly, in python, type:
import matplotlib
